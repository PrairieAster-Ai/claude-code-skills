Quick Start Free

Works with any Claude account — no Pro plan needed.

  1. Download both files using the buttons above
  2. Go to claude.ai and start a new conversation
  3. Attach 3 files to your first message: SKILL-webchat.md, generate_word_docs_web.py, and your resume (.docx or .pdf)
  4. Paste the job description into the message body and add: "I've attached my resume and a job description. I've also attached two instruction files: SKILL-webchat.md contains your workflow instructions, and generate_word_docs_web.py is the Python script for generating .docx files. Please follow the SKILL-webchat.md instructions to assess my fit and generate application documents."
  5. Send — Claude reads the instructions and runs the full workflow
Tip: save these 2 skill files somewhere easy to find — you'll attach them each time you apply to a new job. After your first run, you'll also have a profile.yaml that replaces your resume, so it stays fast.

Better Experience Pro

With a Claude Pro plan you can create a Project so the skill files stay loaded across conversations — no re-uploading.

  1. Download both files using the buttons above
  2. Go to claude.ai and create a new Project
  3. Upload SKILL-webchat.md as Project instructions (or paste its contents into the custom instructions field)
  4. Add generate_word_docs_web.py to Project knowledge (upload the file)
  5. Start a conversation — just attach your resume and paste a job description

Your First Prompt

Here's my resume and a job description. Please assess my fit and generate application documents.

Attach your resume (.docx or .pdf) and paste the full job description. The skill extracts the role and company automatically.

What You Get

Fit Score

Percentage score with a recommendation tier (Strong Fit / Good Fit / Stretch / Poor Fit) plus strengths and gaps breakdown

Tailored Resume

ATS-optimized .docx resume rewritten for the specific job

Cover Letter

Matching .docx cover letter that addresses your specific gaps

Interview Prep

Tips on what to emphasize and which gaps to prepare for

How It Works

  • You upload your resume and paste a job description
  • Claude scores your fit, shows strengths and gaps, and asks if you want to proceed
  • You say "Yes"
  • Claude generates .docx cover letter + resume for download

3–4 messages total per application.

Returning Users

After your first run, Claude offers a profile.yaml file. Save it. Next time, upload profile.yaml instead of your resume to skip parsing and save a message.

Troubleshooting

  • If document generation fails, Claude will offer to output your documents as formatted text you can copy into Word.
  • Use .docx for best results — large PDF files may not parse correctly.
